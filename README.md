<h1>Introduction
</h1>
<p>Welcome to Pashm Saz!
We are students from the University of Würzburg, and we’re excited to share what we’ve created. In this app, you can design your own avatar and customize its appearance with various outfits, accessories like mustaches, glasses, and hats. You can also bring your avatar to life by selecting different animations – whether it’s dancing, doing push-ups, or moving specific body parts. You can even change its color! Once you're done, save your creation to use it later. Have fun exploring all the possibilities!</p>

- **Assets:** Contains images, 3D models, and animations. Key folders include adaKing, AddOns, and ANIMATION for managing assets like models and clips.
- **ExtendebleSaveSystem:** Scripts and examples for saving/loading game data.
- **FST:** Fantasy-themed assets (e.g., wings) with models, prefabs, textures, and demo scripts.
- **Icones and Pics:** A collection of .png images for character customization and UI elements.
- **InkPainter:** Tools for real-time texture painting, including shaders and scripts.
- **MiniSimpleCharachterDemo:** Demo showcasing character models and interactions.
- **Music:** Audio files used for background music or effects.
- **Scripts:** Core C# scripts managing animations, camera, and save/load functionality.
- **UI and ToolbarMenuScript:** Assets for in-game UI and menu creation using Unity’s UI Toolkit. 
- **TextMesh Pro:** Tools for rendering high-quality text.

# How-To

**By clicking on "Help" Button, you will receive complete instructions for the game.**

- **Reset Avatar:** Feature restores your character's appearance and setting to their default state.
- **Reset Camera:** Feature returns the camera view to its default position and angle.
- **Save:** Save your personalize character for later or to share it with others.
- **Export:** Export you personalize character into a 3D environment.

**By diving in on Menu on your leftside, you are getting into custmazing your character.**
- **Hammer Button:** offers a wide selection of accessories for hat, glasses, clothing and hairstyles.
- **Animation Button:** Animating the whole character and adjusting the scale of different body parts.
- **Color Button:** Coloring your character in a differnet and creative ways.
